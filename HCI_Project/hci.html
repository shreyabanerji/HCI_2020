Placement Portal

Introduction

This application helps students to access information regarding placements. The application can be accessed by any student of the organisation with valid login details. The portal helps the students to see the placement schedule, register for companies, check their status, test schedule and all other important information. The aim is to provide all the relevant data to students in a hassle-free manner. In these times, especially when the whole process is being conducted online, it often becomes difficult for students to keep track of the schedule and they often end up missing the deadline to register for a few companies. This portal can be a one stop solution for conduction of online placements.
